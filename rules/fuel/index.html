Fuel

Fuel is stored in tanks and is used on a per-day basis.
At the end of every day you use fuel equal to your Used Resources for power.
If you are using more than one fuel and/or power plant this is a little more complicated, see Multiple Power Plants.

Fuel Types

There are 5 fuel types which are roughly on a scale of power density/efficiency from least power dense at the top to most power dense at the bottom:

  • Hydrogen
  • Lithium Ion
  • Ethanol
  • Methane
  • Nuclear
